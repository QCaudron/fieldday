Join our Field Day#

This year, you can reserve a slot operating any of our stations. Simply grab a time using the forms below, and we’ll see you there!

Running a station#

If you’re a licensed operator, you can sign up to run any of our stations. We’ll have a station manager on-site to help you get set up and answer any questions you might have.

  • Phone / Voice HF station
  • Digital / CW station

GOTA#

If you don’t have a license, or you’re new to radio, you can sign up for a slot on our Get On The Air (GOTA) station. A licensed operator will sit with you and guide you through your first exchanges.

Organization and help#

If you’d like to help out with the organization of the event, we’d love your help. We need folk to help with setup, teardown, cooking, security, and general organization. If you’d like to help out, please let us know here.

Questions ?#

If you have any questions, please get in touch.
